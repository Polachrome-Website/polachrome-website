    Overflowing camera roll? Say no more. Never lose a moment again with the Polaroid Lab. This unique system turns the digital moments in your phone into real-life Polaroid photographs you can hold, keep, or share. An instant formula for timeless Polaroid photographs.

    The Polaroid Lab works with these phone models:

    • iPhone:
      iPhone 7 through iPhone 13 included with IOS 12.4 or higher are compatible with the Polaroid Lab. The augmented reality feature is available with iPhone 7 and newer.

    • Android:
      The Polaroid Lab works with most popular Android phones, such as Samsung, Google Pixel, Huawei, OnePlus, Xiaomi and LG with a screen size bigger than 4.7 inches and 326 ppi density. The Polaroid Lab works with Android 7 and newer.
